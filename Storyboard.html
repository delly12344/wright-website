Website Theme & Design Philosophy

The website embraces a subtle yet personal aesthetic, utilizing a sophisticated color palette of deep greens (#011910), neutral grays (#595959), and earthy tones (rgb(153, 153, 102)). This color scheme reflects both professional maturity and natural authenticity.

The consistent use of spade symbols (♠) and other symbols, as decorative elements adds a unique personal touch while maintaining professional sophistication. The layout prioritizes clear navigation and content accessibility, creating a balanced showcase of both professional achievements and personal development.
